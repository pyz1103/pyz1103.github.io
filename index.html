Game Analysis of Traffic Conflict between Pedestrian and Vehicles on Unsignalized Road Section Based on Cumulative Prospect Theory

Based on cumulative prospect theory and conformity effect, we propose a set of game strategies between pedestrians and vehicles to avoid traffic accidents in unsignal controlled sections.
Yuzhuang Pian, Jinshuan Peng, Lunhui Xu, Pan Wu, Jinlong Li
September 2021 In ICTETS
Analysis and Simulation Optimization of Passenger Flow in Urban Rail Transit Station

According to the characteristics of passenger gathering and evacuation, a passenger traffic behavior model based on social forces is established to find the key bottleneck of passenger flow.

We propose a combined prediction model of extreme learning machine based on artificial bee colony optimization to improve the prediction accuracy of bus travel time.

In this paper, an approach combined with deep learning, namely att-based LSTM (Att-LSTM) model, is proposed to predict short-term traffic speed on urban roads.
